Use **categories** on the Help home page to browse topics. Guides are written for **busy shop people**: short steps first, plain words.

If you are **logged in**, open **Dashboard → Support** for search and quick links to the same articles.

**Who reads what:** owners often start with **Getting started**, **Billing**, and **Staff**. People on the till often need **POS** guides.
