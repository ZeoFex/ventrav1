excerpt: See sales, record expenses, and use exports or scheduled summaries for your books.
order: 10
---

- **Sales views** show what you sold. You can narrow by product, who rang up the sale, and dates. What each person sees depends on their **role**—for example a cashier may not see another branch’s full totals.
- **Expenses** are for day-to-day spending (transport, small supplies, etc.). Use **categories** so end-of-month totals are clear without digging through loose notes.
- **Exports and scheduled reports** (where your plan includes them) let you download figures or get summaries by email—useful if your accountant wants files or you do not log in every day.
