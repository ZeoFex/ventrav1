1. Sign up with an **email you can open** (and add a phone number if the app asks for SMS codes).
2. When prompted, enter the **verification code** from your email or SMS before you rely on the account for a busy sale day.
3. Use **Forgot password** if you need a new password. **Invite each staff member with their own email**—do not share the owner inbox as “the shop login.”

Keep **password reset** access somewhere the trusted owner can find it—not only on a scrap paper customers can see.
