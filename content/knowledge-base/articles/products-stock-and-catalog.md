1. **Product codes (SKU / barcode).** Use one code per product (or per size/colour). Duplicates confuse imports and search—fixing them later is slow.
2. **Opening stock.** Enter stock levels honestly. If the system says you have items you do not, sales and “low stock” warnings will lie to you.
3. **Variations.** If you sell the same item in multiple sizes or colours, add them as **variations** with the right barcode on each. That matches what is on the shelf and what the cashier scans.
4. **Categories and tags.** Pick short names your staff actually use—these power search and filters on the POS.
5. **Low stock.** Check the inventory / warnings area regularly. Alerts help, but you still want to **look at the shelf** sometimes.
