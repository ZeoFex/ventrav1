**Hold sale** saves the basket so you can serve someone else or fix a shelf issue:

1. On **New sale**, add the items to the cart, then choose **Hold sale** from the cart area.
2. Open **Held sales** from POS to see parked baskets.
3. **Resume** brings the basket back so you can take payment; **discard** if the customer cancelled.

**Important:** On many setups today, held carts are saved in **this browser on this device** only. If someone clears browsing data/cookies or you switch to another computer, held sales there can disappear. For training: do not wipe the browser during a shift if you rely on holds, and reopen the **same browser tab/session** until those customers pay.
