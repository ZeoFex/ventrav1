Your **plan** controls what features you can use (reports depth, integrations, support level, and similar). Exact limits depend on what you bought—check **Billing** or **Settings** in the app for your business.

**Practical tips**

- Keep an eye on **trial** end dates so you renew before the team hits a locked screen at checkout.
- After you pay through **Paystack**, give the system a short time to update. If your status still says pending, open **Billing** / subscription area (or ask your finance person) to match the payment to the business.
- If you think you were **charged twice**, contact support with the **receipt or transaction reference** from Paystack or your bank SMS—so support can check the right payment and not guess.
