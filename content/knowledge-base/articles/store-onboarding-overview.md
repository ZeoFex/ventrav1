When you first open the dashboard, follow the **onboarding** steps in order. Most shops touch:

- **Branches** — at least one location (you can rename it later).
- **Products, categories, tags** — add enough real items to **practice a sale**, even if barcodes and labels come later.
- **How you get paid** — set this in **Finance** / billing so checkout matches what you use (card, mobile money, etc., depending on your provider and country).
- **Staff** — invite people and assign **cashier** vs **manager** (or similar) so only trusted people can void sales or change the catalogue.

You do not need every optional field on day one. Add tax details, **product codes**, and tighter stock discipline as you grow—that is easier than cleaning months of sloppy data later.
