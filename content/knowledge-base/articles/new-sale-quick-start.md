---
title: New sale — basics for the cashier
slug: new-sale-quick-start
category: pos
excerpt: Add items to the cart, fix quantities, take payment, and hand over a receipt.
order: 10
---

1. Open **Dashboard → POS → New sale** (exact wording may match your sidebar).
2. Browse **categories** or use **search**; tap products to add to the cart. Use **+** and **−** for quantities.
3. When the **total** looks right, go to **payment**—glance at tax rows if your business shows them.
4. Pick how the customer pays: **cash**, **card**, **mobile money**, or **split payment**—only options your boss enabled will appear.
5. Give or send the **receipt** however your shop does it (print, SMS, email, screenshot).

**Held sales:** you can **park** a cart (held sale) while the buyer steps away—but on many setups that lives **only in this browser** until they pay. Finish held sales before clearing site data or changing device; see **Held sales** in Help for detail.
