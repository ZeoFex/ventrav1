A **branch** is simply another shop or outlet under the **same business** in VentraPOS—for example your main stall and your weekend market table, still one owner.

- **Stock per shop.** Each branch can keep its own opening stock so totals reflect **that** shop, not a mix-up with another place.
- **Prices.** Charge the **same** price everywhere, or **different** prices per branch if costs or customer expectations differ.
- **Moving stock between shops.** When goods move from one branch to another, staff should **record the move** and agree on quantities—so stock on screen matches the floor.
- **Reports across branches.** If you combine all shops in a report, each site may **end the trading day** at a different clock time (or time zone). “Today” may not line up until everyone follows the closing rule you care about.

Open **Branches** in your dashboard for your setup details, or ask whoever administers your account.
